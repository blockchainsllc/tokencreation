Pressing the "Send Transaction" button will sign and broadcast an ETH transaction with your newly created account for the pledged amount. This signed transaction will Create DAO tokens as soon the balance of your newly created account {{account.adr}} exceeds the pledged amount of ETH. To fully participate in the DAO this account will need hold a small balance of ETH. We recommend you send at least 0.1 ETH extra.

Note that this does not lock in the current price of the DAO tokens. The price of the DAO tokens will be the price at the time the ETH is sent to your newly created account. If you send ETH to this account after the sale has ended, you will not receive any DAO Tokens.

Step 3 : Create Tokens

There are multiple exchanges implemented Code in order Create and trade Tokens for the Generic DAO-Framework. You can use

  1. Bittrex
  2. Gatecoin
    On Gatecoin you may register as a user and then be able to Create DAO tokens there. In order to use them for voting you may transafer the ownership to an account you can control.

In order to obtian DAO tokens using US Dollars, Euros, or Swiss Francs, please continue at Bity.com. They have integrated the generic DAO framework and have built a custom interface for the Creation of new DAOs.

  1. Proceed to Bity.com
  2. Create a new account
  3. Confirm your email and Login
  4. Enter your phone number and verify your account
  5. Register your Ethereum account's address
  6. Follow the instructions
  7. Enter the amount you want to use to Create DAO tokens. To spend more than 250 EUR, USD or CHF you will have to further verify your identity with Bity.
  8. Select bank transfer
  9. Follow the instructions to complete the payment and be sure to include the reference code when creating the bank transfer
