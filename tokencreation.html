Step 1 : Choose your Currency!

Select the currency you are using to Create DAO tokens. The DAO lives on the Ethereum blockchain, for this reason it can only directly accept ETH, any other currency will be converted to ETH before being sent to the DAO.

ETH (No extra conversions required) Choose this if you want to use ETH that you already own and you want to securely hold the DAO tokens yourself. BTC (using Gatecoin on this site) Choose this if you want to use BTC directly on this website enabled by Gatecoin and you want to securely hold the DAO tokens yourself. BTC or other digital currencies (using a ShapeShift window) Choose this if you want to use any digital currencies ShapeShift supports and you want to securely hold the DAO tokens yourself. BTC, USD, EUR or CHF (using external sites Bity, Bittrex or Gatecoin) Choose this if you want to use national currencies, or you would like to hold your DAO tokens on an exchange website.

Pressing the "Sign Transaction" button will sign and broadcast an ETH transaction with your newly created account for the pledged amount. This signed transaction will Create DAO tokens as soon the balance of your newly generated account {{account.adr}} exceeds the pledged amount of ETH. To fully participate in the DAO this account will need hold a small balance of ETH. We recommend you send at least 0.1 ETH extra.

Note that this does not lock in the current Creation rate of the DAO tokens. The Creation rate of DAO tokens will be the rate at the time the ETH is sent to your newly created account. If you send ETH to this account after the Creation phase has ended, you will not receive any DAO Tokens.

Step 3 : Create Tokens

Multiple exchanges have integrated with the Standard DAO Framework to enable you to Create and trade DAO tokens directly on their exchange. You can use:

  1. Bittrex (Global support, including New York residents)
    On Bittrex you will have to create a new account or sign in as a returning user before you will be able to Create DAO tokens. You will not own the tokens you Create using this method; they will be held directly on the exchange. In order to truly own DAO tokens and use them to vote or participate in the inner workings of The DAO in any way, you will have to install the Ethereum Wallet and withdraw your DAO tokens to your Ethereum Wallet account.
  2. Gatecoin (Does not support US Residents)
    On Gatecoin you will have to create a new account or sign in as a returning user before you will be able to Create DAO tokens. You will not own the tokens you Create using this method; they will be held directly on the exchange. In order to truly own DAO tokens and use them to vote or participate in the inner workings of The DAO in any way, you will have to install the Ethereum Wallet and withdraw your DAO tokens to your Ethereum Wallet account.

In order to create DAO tokens using US dollars, euros, or Swiss francs, please continue to Bity.com and just follow these simple steps:

  1. Sign in or create a new Bity account
  2. Open an order for DAO tokens(*) and follow the on-screen instructions. (*) Please note that to convert more than 250 EUR, USD or CHF you will have to verify your identity with Bity. Once logged in, this straightforward process can be found here: https://bity.com/dashboard/kyc
  3. Execute the bank transfer associated to your order to finalize the transaction

(*) Please note that to convert more than 250 EUR, USD or CHF you will have to verify your identity with Bity. Once logged in, this straightforward process can be found here: https://bity.com/dashboard/kyc
